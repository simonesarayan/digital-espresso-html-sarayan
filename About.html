Arabica
Arabica beans are known for their smooth, mild flavor and pleasant aroma. They often have hints of sweetness, fruit, or chocolate, making them one of the most popular and high-quality coffee varieties.
Robusta
Robusta beans have a stronger, bolder taste with a more bitter and earthy flavor. They contain more caffeine than Arabica and are commonly used in espresso blends for a richer, fuller body.
Fair Trade
Fair Trade coffee is sourced from farmers who are paid fair wages and work under ethical conditions. Choosing Fair Trade supports sustainable farming practices and helps improve the livelihoods of coffee-growing communities.
